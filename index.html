Welcome to the webpage for the Shopper App, where shopping is made ethical!

The app and website are still under construction, but we thought we could give you an overview of what to expect:

  • See how ethical a product is when you scan its QR code.
  • See any relevant news headlines associated with the ethicality of the product
  • Shop for alternative ethical products on our app
  • Submit product entries for us to review

How the ethical ranking works:

  • Ranking is broken down into 4 components: Environmental impact, working conditions, complicity in corruption, and complicity in genocide.
  • Rankings sourced from reputable sources, like the Ethical Consumer, The Business and Human Rights Resource Center , and Amnesty International to name a few. A more comprehensive list will be released soon.
